Idan Achituve

Hi!

I am a PhD student at Bar-Ilan University, supervised jointly by Prof. Gal Chechik from the Gonda Brain research institute and director of AI at NVIDIA, and Prof. Ethan Fetaya from the Faculty of Engineering. My research focus is primarily on machine learning problems in the low data regime. I am interested in developing methods and improve current procedures that combine the elegance of Bayesian modeling with the expressiveness abilities of deep learning models.
I hold a MSc in Computer Science and a BSc in Industrial Engineering. Both with great honor.
Also, I have several years of industry experience, working as a Data Scientist at RSA.
